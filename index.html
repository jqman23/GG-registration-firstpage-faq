We are excited to offer CEUs this year to US-based Licensed Social Workers, Licensed Marriage & Family Therapists, and Licensed Mental Health Counselors. The Kempe Center is accredited by the Washington NASW chapter, meaning CEUs earned by attendees will be accepted in many states across the US.

The majority of sessions are CEU eligible. All session types are able to offer CEU credit aside from Lecture Halls and Facilitated Discussion Groups. At least one CEU-eligible session is offered in each time block.

Your one-time CEU purchase enables you to claim CEUs for CEU-eligible sessions you attend and for which you submit the requisite knowledge surveys.

To purchase CEUs, answer "Yes" when asked "Do you wish to purchase CEUs for this event?" then click the button at the bottom of the page.
