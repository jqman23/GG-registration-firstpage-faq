Lived experience scholarships are also available for people or parents with child protection or child welfare system involvement and people with lived experience in foster care. We are excited to offer CEUs this year to US-based Licensed Social Workers, Licensed Marriage & Family Therapists, and Licensed Mental Health Counselors. The Kempe Center is accredited by the Washington NASW chapter, meaning CEUs earned by attendees will be accepted in many states across the US.

The majority of sessions are CEU eligible. At least one CEU-eligible session is offered in each time block.

Your one-time CEU purchase enables you to claim CEUs for CEU-eligible sessions you attend and for which you submit the requisite knowledge surveys. Please ensure you indicated your interest in claiming CEUs on the previous page.

A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare is a three-day virtual event taking place October 6–8, 2026. Day 1 features Skill Building Institutes, and Days 2–3 feature the Global Gathering program, including keynotes, workshops, strategy sessions, creative spaces, and opportunities for connection.

View the full program.

Skill Building Institutes are immersive, hands-on learning experiences offered on Day 1. These 3½-hour sessions are designed for practical skill development, deeper learning, and applied frameworks that support future-focused child welfare practice and leadership.

Learn more about Skill Building Institutes.
